## Short Public Description
**The Light Hub is a youth-focused innovation and technology hub dedicated to empowering young people with skills, mentorship, and resources in Web3, blockchain, and Fourth Industrial Revolution technologies.**  
Through hands-on learning, experimentation, and community-driven projects, The Light Hub supports the creation of impactful solutions and sustainable livelihoods.

---

## Focus Areas
- **Blockchain & Web3** (dApps, smart contracts, tokenization concepts)
- **4IR Technologies** (AI foundations, IoT, automation, data systems)
- **Youth Innovation & Digital Skills**
- **Community Impact Projects** (solutions aligned to real-world local challenges)
- **Open-source Collaboration** (reusable tools, templates, and learning kits)
